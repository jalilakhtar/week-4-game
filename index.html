Crystal Collector Game

How to Play this Game


  1. You are given a "Random Number "at the start of the game
  2. There are four crystals:
    • Blue
    • Red
    • Yellow
    • Green
  3. By clicking on a crystal image, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. If you match your total score to the Random Number, you win.
  5. If your total score is more than the random number, you lose.
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  7. The values of each crystal changes with each game start.
